Our Story

LML Arts & Sign began in 1996, founded by Lito Lacanlale started as a small family business from a modest space along McArthur Highway in Mabalacat, Pampanga. At first, we focused on manually crafting banners and streamers, utilizing paint and the skilled hands of our team. Recognizing the need to adapt to industry advancements, we gradually invested in modern tarpaulin printing machines, printers, and other equipment to better meet the evolving needs of our customers.

As our business grew, we expanded to operate three branches in Mabalacat. Our journey from a small operation to a leader in the printing industry showcases our commitment to combining traditional techniques with modern technology. Over the years, we transitioned from manual printing methods to high-tech presses, staying competitive and responsive to customer demands.

Today, we continue to focus on innovation and excellence, operating from a larger commercial space in Dau. At LML Arts & Sign, we understand that our success is linked to our clients’ success. We are dedicated to providing top-quality printing services that reflect your company’s image, maintaining a high standard of professionalism and integrity in all we do.
